The Team

Pictured below is our team of development engineers.

All were enrolled in Capstone Design at Oklahoma State University in the Spring of 2016.

Christian Coffield

Christian is responsible for the encryption of all captured photos, as well as transmission between the camera device and the server. He’s also responsible for most documentation of the project, taking the lead editing role and producing content for the website.In his spare time, Christian likes to write stories, play video games, and spend time at home with his fiancé and pets.

Matthew enjoys fitness, film, games, and spending time with his fiancé in his free time as well as his pet tortoise, Dino.

In his free time he enjoys not having a fiancé and working on many side projects.

Kevin Seitz

In addition, he is in charge of packaging for the camera system. In his spare time he enjoys swimming, video games, going to the movie theater, and spending time with his fiancé.
